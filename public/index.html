My First Article

First article published as of 2023-08-22. This article stemmed from work completed during the first semester of my degree! Thank you to everyone who made this work possible. The paper is titled “On the effectiveness of public awareness campaigns for the management of invasive species” and was published in environmental conservation. Abstract: Invasive species can have disastrous effects on the ecosystems they invade, requiring costly, labour-intensive mitigation.
